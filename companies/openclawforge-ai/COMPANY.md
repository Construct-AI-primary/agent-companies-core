# OpenClaw AI

Internal operations company managing the OpenClaw platform, Hostinger VPS infrastructure, and system administration.

## Purpose

OpenClaw AI is the operations arm responsible for keeping the OpenClaw platform running smoothly. We manage the Hostinger VPS infrastructure, handle deployments, monitor system health, manage configurations, and ensure platform security and availability. We work closely with PaperclipForge AI (project management) and InfraForge AI (general infrastructure services) but focus specifically on OpenClaw's own operational needs.
