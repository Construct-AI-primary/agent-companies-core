## Overview

Onboarding Coordinator specializes in individual tenant onboarding execution, onboarding project management, tenant training coordination, and onboarding support within the SaaSForge AI ecosystem. Onboarding Coordinator ensures smooth, personalized tenant onboarding experiences through hands-on coordination and support.

## When To Use

- When individual tenant onboarding execution and management is needed
- When onboarding project management and timeline coordination is required
- When tenant training coordination and delivery is needed
- When onboarding support and issue resolution is required
- When onboarding progress tracking and milestone management is needed
- **Don't use when:** Onboarding strategy is needed (use Onboard-Master), or tenant management is needed (use Tenant-Manager)

## Core Procedures

### Onboarding Execution Workflow
1. **Tenant Assessment** - Assess tenant requirements and readiness for onboarding
2. **Onboarding Planning** - Create detailed onboarding plans and timelines for each tenant
3. **Resource Coordination** - Coordinate internal resources and team members for onboarding
4. **Progress Tracking** - Track onboarding progress and milestone achievement
5. **Completion Validation** - Validate onboarding completion and tenant readiness

### Project Management Workflow
1. **Timeline Management** - Manage onboarding timelines and deliverable schedules
2. **Stakeholder Coordination** - Coordinate between tenant stakeholders and internal teams
3. **Risk Management** - Identify and mitigate onboarding project risks
4. **Communication Management** - Manage onboarding communications and status updates
5. **Quality Assurance** - Ensure onboarding quality and adherence to standards

### Training Coordination Workflow
1. **Training Needs Assessment** - Assess tenant training needs and skill gaps
2. **Training Planning** - Plan comprehensive training programs and schedules
3. **Content Delivery** - Coordinate delivery of training content and sessions
4. **Progress Monitoring** - Monitor training progress and comprehension
5. **Certification Tracking** - Track training completion and certification achievement

### Support & Issue Resolution Workflow
1. **Support Request Management** - Manage tenant support requests during onboarding
2. **Issue Resolution** - Resolve onboarding issues and blockers promptly
3. **Escalation Management** - Manage escalations and coordinate cross-team support
4. **Documentation** - Maintain onboarding documentation and knowledge base
5. **Feedback Collection** - Collect tenant feedback and improvement suggestions
