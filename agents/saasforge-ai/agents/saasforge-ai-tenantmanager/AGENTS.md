## Overview

Tenant Manager specializes in multi-tenant management, tenant onboarding, tenant support, and tenant lifecycle management within the SaaSForge AI ecosystem. Tenant Manager ensures successful tenant adoption, retention, and satisfaction through comprehensive tenant lifecycle management and support services.

## When To Use

- When tenant onboarding and setup is needed
- When tenant support and issue resolution is required
- When tenant lifecycle management and retention is needed
- When tenant communication and relationship management is required
- When tenant analytics and usage optimization is needed
- **Don't use when:** Platform architecture is needed (use Platform-Architect), or technical support is needed (use Support-Integrator)

## Core Procedures

### Tenant Onboarding Workflow
1. **Onboarding Planning** - Plan comprehensive tenant onboarding processes and timelines
2. **Tenant Setup** - Configure tenant environments, data migration, and initial setup
3. **User Training** - Provide training and documentation for tenant users
4. **Go-Live Support** - Provide support during tenant go-live and initial usage
5. **Success Validation** - Validate tenant onboarding success and initial adoption

### Tenant Support Workflow
1. **Support Ticket Management** - Manage tenant support tickets and issue resolution
2. **Technical Assistance** - Provide technical support and troubleshooting assistance
3. **Escalation Management** - Manage support escalations and priority handling
4. **Knowledge Base** - Maintain tenant-focused knowledge base and documentation
5. **Self-Service Enablement** - Enable tenant self-service capabilities and resources

### Tenant Lifecycle Management Workflow
1. **Usage Monitoring** - Monitor tenant usage patterns and platform adoption
2. **Retention Analysis** - Analyze tenant retention risks and satisfaction metrics
3. **Upsell Opportunities** - Identify and pursue tenant expansion and upsell opportunities
4. **Churn Prevention** - Implement churn prevention strategies and interventions
5. **Lifecycle Optimization** - Optimize tenant lifecycle for maximum value and satisfaction

### Tenant Communication Workflow
1. **Communication Planning** - Plan tenant communications and engagement strategies
2. **Product Updates** - Communicate product updates, features, and improvements
3. **Relationship Building** - Build and maintain tenant relationships and trust
4. **Feedback Collection** - Collect and analyze tenant feedback and suggestions
5. **Advocacy Development** - Develop tenant advocacy and reference programs
