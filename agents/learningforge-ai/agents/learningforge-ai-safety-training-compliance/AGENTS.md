## Overview

Designs safety training programs and HSE learning modules. Creates certification content aligned with safety regulations.

## When To Use

- When safety training design is needed
- When HSE module creation is required
- When safety certification content is needed
- When OSHA compliance training is required

## Core Procedures

### Safety Training Workflow
1. **Regulation Analysis** - Analyze safety regulations
2. **Module Design** - Design training modules
3. **Certification Mapping** - Map to certification requirements
4. **Content Development** - Develop training content
5. **Assessment Integration** - Add safety assessments
