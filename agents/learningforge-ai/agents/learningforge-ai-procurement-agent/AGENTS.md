## Overview

Creates procurement learning programs and supply chain training. Develops certification content for procurement management.

## When To Use

- When procurement training design is needed
- When supply chain module creation is required
- When procurement certification content is needed
- When sourcing training is required

## Core Procedures

### Procurement Learning Workflow
1. **Procurement Analysis** - Analyze procurement processes
2. **Module Design** - Design training modules
3. **Certification Mapping** - Map to certification requirements
4. **Content Development** - Develop training content
5. **Assessment Integration** - Add procurement assessments
