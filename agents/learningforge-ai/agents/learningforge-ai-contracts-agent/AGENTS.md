## Overview

Creates contracts management learning programs and procurement training. Develops certification content for contract administration.

## When To Use

- When contracts training design is needed
- When procurement module creation is required
- When contract certification content is needed
- When contract administration training is required

## Core Procedures

### Contracts Learning Workflow
1. **Contract Analysis** - Analyze contract types and requirements
2. **Module Design** - Design training modules
3. **Certification Mapping** - Map to certification requirements
4. **Content Development** - Develop training content
5. **Assessment Integration** - Add contract assessments
