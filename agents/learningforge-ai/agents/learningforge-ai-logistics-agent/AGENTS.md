## Overview

Creates logistics and supply chain learning programs. Develops certification content for logistics management.

## When To Use

- When logistics training design is needed
- When supply chain module creation is required
- When logistics certification content is needed
- When inventory management training is required

## Core Procedures

### Logistics Learning Workflow
1. **Logistics Analysis** - Analyze logistics processes
2. **Module Design** - Design training modules
3. **Certification Mapping** - Map to certification requirements
4. **Content Development** - Develop training content
5. **Assessment Integration** - Add logistics assessments
