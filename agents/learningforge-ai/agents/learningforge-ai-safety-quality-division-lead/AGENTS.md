## Overview

Creates quality control learning programs and QA training modules. Develops certification content for quality assurance.

## When To Use

- When quality training design is needed
- When QA module creation is required
- When quality certification content is needed
- When quality standards training is required

## Core Procedures

### Quality Learning Workflow
1. **Standards Analysis** - Analyze quality standards
2. **Module Design** - Design training modules
3. **Certification Mapping** - Map to certification requirements
4. **Content Development** - Develop training content
5. **Assessment Integration** - Add quality assessments
